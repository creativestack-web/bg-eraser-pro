Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AI background removal, answered clearly and honestly.

Is BG Eraser completely free to use?

Yes — removing an image background is entirely free for every user. You can upload your photo, process it with our AI, and download the full transparent PNG result at no cost and without creating an account. BG Eraser is funded through non-intrusive advertising rather than user subscriptions, meaning the core tool remains genuinely accessible to everyone regardless of budget.

Which image formats does BG Eraser support?

BG Eraser accepts PNG, JPG, JPEG, SVG, and WebP image files up to 10 MB in size. The underlying remove.bg API processes images up to 25 megapixels in resolution. For optimal edge accuracy, we recommend using photos with clear contrast between the subject and background, though the AI model handles even cluttered or complex scenes with impressive fidelity.

How does the AI background removal actually work?

BG Eraser uses a convolutional neural network (CNN) trained on millions of images spanning humans, animals, products, vehicles, and more. When you upload a photo, the model performs semantic segmentation: it classifies every pixel as either "subject" or "background" and generates a high-resolution alpha-matte mask. This mask is applied to produce a crisp transparent PNG. All computation runs on cloud infrastructure, which is why results arrive in seconds rather than minutes.

Are my uploaded images stored or shared anywhere?

No. Privacy is non-negotiable. Images you upload are transmitted securely over HTTPS directly to the remove.bg processing API and are never stored on any BG Eraser server. Remove.bg's own privacy policy states images are deleted from their infrastructure within 24 hours of processing. We never sell, share, analyse, or use your uploaded photos for any commercial purpose whatsoever.

Will there be a watermark on my downloaded image?

Absolutely not. The PNG file you download contains only your subject isolated on a transparent background — clean, professional, and ready for commercial or personal use without any BG Eraser branding. The only technical caveat is that extremely high-megapixel images may be returned at a preview resolution on the free API tier; for guaranteed full-resolution output on very large photos, a paid remove.bg plan may be required.

Can I remove backgrounds from portraits, products, and animals?

Yes. The AI model is trained on an extremely diverse range of subject types. It excels at portrait and headshot photography (including fine hair detail), product and lifestyle shots, pet and wildlife photos, car and vehicle images, and logos on flat backgrounds. Highly transparent objects like glass, mirrors, or crystal may require manual refinement afterward, as fully transparent surfaces present a universal challenge for automated segmentation tools.

Can I use the resulting transparent image commercially?

BG Eraser imposes no additional copyright restrictions on processed output — the result belongs entirely to you and may be used for any purpose, including commercial work. However, you remain solely responsible for ensuring you hold the rights to the original source image. If you are the photographer or hold valid licensing for the photo, you are free to use the background-removed version however you choose, including in product listings, advertising, and client deliverables.

How do I add a new background after removing the original?

After downloading your transparent PNG, open it in any design tool — Adobe Photoshop, Canva (free, browser-based), Figma, GIMP (free, open-source), or even Google Slides. Place any colour, gradient, photograph, or pattern behind your subject layer. Because the PNG uses a genuine alpha channel, the composite looks completely natural with no edge fringing, halos, or artefacts. Canva is particularly recommended for non-designers as it is intuitive, free, and requires no software installation.

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how BG Eraser Pro, published and operated by CreativeStack (creativestack-web) at https://creativestack-web.github.io/bg-eraser-pro/ ("the Website", "the Tool", "we", "our", or "us"), collects, uses, and safeguards information when you access our service.

BG Eraser Pro is a free, browser-based AI image background removal tool. By using the Website, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please discontinue use of the Website immediately.

Plain-Language Summary

We do not sell your data. We do not store your images. We use standard website analytics and display Google ads. You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time. Your images are processed in real time and immediately discarded after delivery of results.

01

Information We Collect

BG Eraser Pro collects the minimum information necessary to operate the service and deliver a high-quality user experience. Information is collected in two primary ways:

1.1 Information You Provide Voluntarily

When you use BG Eraser Pro's background removal feature, you upload an image file from your device. This image is the only content you actively submit to the tool. It is transmitted over an encrypted HTTPS connection to our third-party image processing API (remove.bg) solely for the purpose of background removal. We do not receive, store, cache, or retain a copy of this image on any BG Eraser Pro server.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

Like the vast majority of websites on the internet, BG Eraser Pro automatically collects certain non-personally identifiable technical information when you visit the Website. This may include:

  • Your Internet Protocol (IP) address
  • Browser type, version, and language settings
  • Operating system and device type
  • Referring URLs and exit pages
  • Date, time, and duration of your visit
  • Pages and features accessed during your session
  • Geographic region (country/city level, derived from IP address)

This information is collected through standard web server log files and third-party analytics services. It is used in aggregate form to understand how users interact with the Website and to improve performance and usability.

No Account Required

BG Eraser Pro does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or submit any personally identifiable information to use the background removal feature. You are anonymous to us.

02

Image Processing — Critical Disclosure

We Do NOT Store Your Images — Ever

Images uploaded to BG Eraser Pro are processed exclusively in real time via a secure, encrypted connection to the remove.bg API. At no point are your uploaded images saved, stored, archived, backed up, cached, indexed, or retained on any BG Eraser Pro or CreativeStack server, database, cloud storage bucket, or other persistent storage medium.

The complete lifecycle of an image uploaded to BG Eraser Pro is as follows:

Step Action Storage Impact
1 You select or drag an image file in your browser. File exists only in your browser's local memory (RAM). No upload has occurred.
2 You click "Remove Background". The browser sends a direct HTTPS API request to remove.bg containing your image. The image is transmitted over TLS encryption. It is not routed through any BG Eraser Pro server.
3 The remove.bg neural network processes the image and returns a transparent PNG result. remove.bg processes the image in memory and deletes it within 24 hours per their own privacy policy.
4 The transparent PNG result is delivered directly to your browser. The result is stored only in your browser's temporary memory until you download it or close the tab.
5 You click "Download HD PNG" to save the result. The file is saved to your local device. It is removed from browser memory when the tab is closed or you upload a new image.

Third-Party API Processing — remove.bg

Background removal is powered by the remove.bg API, operated by Kaleido AI GmbH, Schottenfeldgasse 26/2/31, 1070 Vienna, Austria. Remove.bg has its own privacy policy governing its handling of submitted images, which is available at https://www.remove.bg/privacy. We encourage you to review their policy. By using BG Eraser Pro, you also agree to be bound by remove.bg's terms of service with respect to image processing.

Remove.bg states that images uploaded via API are automatically and permanently deleted from their servers within 24 hours of processing. They do not use uploaded images to train machine learning models without explicit consent.

Cookies & Web Beacons

BG Eraser Pro uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your browsing experience, deliver relevant advertisements, and analyse website traffic. This section explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can control them.

What Are Cookies?

A cookie is a small text file that a website places on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit it. Cookies allow websites to "remember" information about your visit, such as your preferred language settings or whether you have previously agreed to a terms of service banner. Cookies do not contain executable code, viruses, or any personally identifiable information unless you have specifically provided such information to the website.

What Are Web Beacons?

A web beacon (also called a pixel tag, clear GIF, or tracking pixel) is a tiny, transparent 1×1 pixel image embedded in a web page or email. When your browser loads a page containing a web beacon, it sends a request to the server hosting the image. This request can record information such as your IP address, the time of your visit, and whether you performed a specific action (such as clicking a link). Web beacons are commonly used by advertising networks to track the effectiveness of ad campaigns.

Google DoubleClick DART Cookies

BG Eraser Pro uses Google AdSense to serve advertisements. As part of this advertising relationship, Google uses a technology known as the DoubleClick DART cookie to serve ads to visitors of BG Eraser Pro.

What is the DART Cookie?

The DART cookie is set by Google's advertising network (DoubleClick, now part of Google's marketing platform) and enables Google to display advertisements to you on BG Eraser Pro and other websites based on your previous visits to sites across the Internet. This is known as interest-based or behavioural advertising.

How DART Cookies Work

When you visit BG Eraser Pro, the Google AdSense code embedded in our pages instructs your browser to request an ad from Google's ad servers. As part of this process, Google may set or read a DART cookie (IDE) stored in your browser under the domain doubleclick.net. This cookie does not contain personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or postal address.

The DART cookie stores a unique, anonymous identifier (a randomly generated string of characters). Google uses this identifier to:

  • Serve you advertisements that are relevant to your interests, based on your browsing history across participating websites.
  • Limit the number of times you see a particular advertisement (frequency capping).
  • Measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns and report aggregated statistics to advertisers.
  • Prevent you from seeing the same ad repeatedly in a short period.

Opting Out of DART / Personalised Advertising

You can opt out of Google's use of the DART cookie for interest-based advertising at any time. To do so, visit the Google Ad Settings page at https://www.google.com/settings/ads, or opt out through the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page at https://optout.networkadvertising.org/.

Opting out of personalised advertising means you will still see advertisements on BG Eraser Pro, but they will be contextual (based on the content of the page) rather than interest-based (based on your browsing history).

For more information about how Google uses data when you use our site and how to manage Google advertising cookies, please visit: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect the information we process against unauthorised access, accidental loss, alteration, or disclosure.

Security Measures We Implement

  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our Website — including image uploads to the remove.bg API — is protected by TLS (Transport Layer Security) encryption. This prevents interception of data in transit.
  • No Server-Side Image Storage: The most significant security measure is our architectural decision to never store user images on our infrastructure. There is no database, file system, or cloud storage containing your images, which means there is nothing for an adversary to breach.
  • API Key Security: Our remove.bg API key is protected and is never exposed in client-side code that would allow unauthorised parties to use it.
  • GitHub Pages CDN: The Website is served via GitHub's global content delivery network, which includes DDoS protection, security monitoring, and automatic HTTPS certificate management.
  • Third-Party Security: We rely on established, security-audited third-party services (Google, GitHub, remove.bg) that maintain their own comprehensive security programmes.
Limitation of Liability

While we implement reasonable security measures, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security. In the event of a data breach that is likely to result in a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify affected parties and relevant supervisory authorities as required by applicable law.
